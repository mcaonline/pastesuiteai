Licensing & Trial

Trial, free tier, and license activation

Overview

PasteSuiteAI offers a generous trial and a permanent free tier. Non-AI features — such as Paste as Plain Text, local regex transforms, and static text paste — are always free with no usage limits, regardless of your license status.

Phase Duration AI Actions Notes
Trial 7 days Unlimited Starts on first launch. No sign-up required.
Free Indefinite 15 per rolling 24h window Escalating cooldowns as limit approaches.
Licensed As long as license is valid Unlimited Key stored in OS keychain.

Trial Period

A 7-day trial starts automatically on the first launch of PasteSuiteAI. During the trial:

  • All AI features are available without any restrictions.
  • No credit card or account is required.
  • No upsell prompts appear.

After the trial ends, the app automatically transitions to the Free tier. The trial start date is stored securely in your OS credential store.

Free Tier

After the trial expires, you can continue using AI features within a rolling 24-hour usage window:

  • 15 AI actions per rolling 24-hour window.
  • Brief cooldowns between actions as the limit approaches.
  • Cooldowns reset automatically as older actions age out of the 24-hour window.
Non-AI features (Paste as Plain Text, clipboard history, regex transforms, static text paste, all hotkey and trigger configuration) are always free regardless of your tier.

Pro — Pricing & Subscription

The Pro license is an annual subscription. The current price is €29 per year, incl. VAT where applicable. The subscription renews automatically each year at the then-current price; you can cancel anytime, and your current paid year stays active until its end.

  • Scope of the license: A Pro key is for one single user and may be activated on up to 6 of your personal devices. Re-installing PasteSuiteAI on a device you have already activated is idempotent and does not consume an additional slot. The license is non-transferable and may not be shared with other people.
  • Seller of record: Purchases are processed by Paddle (Paddle.com Market Ltd., United Kingdom / Paddle Inc., USA), which acts as the merchant of record and issues the invoice.
  • Manage or cancel: Use the Paddle Buyer Portal link included in your purchase confirmation email. Your license remains valid for the remainder of the paid year after cancellation.
  • Refunds & right of withdrawal: See the Terms of Service and the withdrawal instructions Paddle provides during checkout.

License Activation

A license key removes the daily AI action limit and cooldown delays. To activate:

  1. Open Settings and go to the License tab.
  2. Enter your license key in the input field. Keys use the format PASTEAI-….
  3. Click Activate or press Enter.
  4. A confirmation message shows your licensed email, tier, and expiry if applicable.
Control Description
License Key input Enter your PASTEAI-… key here. Visible only when not licensed.
Activate button Submits the key for verification. Also triggered by pressing Enter in the input.
Deactivate button Removes the license key from the OS keychain. The app reverts to the free tier. Visible only when licensed.
Pro badge effect toggle Cosmetic option (licensed users only). Enables an animated gradient effect on the Pro badge in the title bar. Default: on.

Technical details about license verification:

  • Online activation. Entering a key requires an internet connection so the key can be activated for your device. After activation, the signed key is cached locally and does not require a constant connection for day-to-day use.
  • Secure storage. The key is stored in your OS secure credential store. It is never written to disk as plain text.
  • Periodic re-verification. The app periodically re-verifies your license. If the key is missing or invalid, the app falls back to the free tier automatically.
License activation is rate-limited to prevent abuse. If you enter too many invalid keys in a short period, wait a moment before trying again.

Paywall Behavior

When you hit the daily AI limit, PasteSuiteAI uses a graduated, non-intrusive approach:

Soft Banner

Appears on the first time you hit the daily limit within a 7-day period. The banner shows at the top of the main panel and is dismissible. It includes:

  • An Upgrade button that switches the banner to inline activation mode, letting you enter a license key without opening Settings.
  • A dismiss button with an optional countdown (you may need to wait a few seconds before dismissing on first appearance).

Strong Modal

Appears on the second or later limit hit within a 7-day window. A more prominent dialog shows usage statistics and a call-to-action to enter a license key. Features:

  • A CTA button that switches to inline activation mode directly in the modal.
  • A dismiss button to close the modal and continue with the free tier.
  • Clicking the backdrop also dismisses the modal.
  • A cooldown between strong paywall appearances prevents it from showing on every single action once the limit is hit.
Non-AI actions are never blocked, regardless of license status or how many limit hits have occurred.

Usage Display

Your current license status is visible in the main window title bar as a compact badge:

  • Trial: shows days remaining (e.g., "6 days left").
  • Free: shows actions used vs. the daily limit (e.g., "8 of 15").
  • Licensed: shows a "Pro" badge with an optional animated gradient effect.

The full breakdown is available in Settings › License: phase, trial days remaining, actions used today, daily limit, licensed email, tier, and license validity period.

Safety Limits

Separate from licensing, PasteSuiteAI includes a daily API call safety limit that applies to all users (trial, free, and licensed). This prevents runaway API costs if an automation bug or long action chain misbehaves.

Setting Default Range
Daily API Call Limit 500 10–100,000

The counter resets at midnight UTC each day. When the limit is reached, all AI actions are blocked until the next UTC day. Configure it in Settings › General › Safety.

The safety limit is independent of the free-tier daily limit. A licensed user with 500 AI calls per day set as the safety limit can still be blocked if they hit 500 calls before midnight UTC.

Non-AI Features (Always Free)

  • Paste as Plain Text — strip formatting and paste clean text.
  • Local Regex Transform — run regex-based text transformations locally.
  • Static Text Paste — paste pre-defined text snippets.
  • Clipboard History — in-session history of clipboard changes and action results.
  • Global hotkeys & mouse triggers — all trigger configuration and assignment.
  • Prompt Library — organize and access prompts via the overlay.
  • All settings & customization — UI scale, themes, connections, actions.
