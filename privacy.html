Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

PasteSuiteAI is a desktop application that runs entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, collect telemetry, or track usage. This policy explains what data the app stores locally and when data leaves your device.

Data Controller

Keystroke Labs is the data controller for PasteSuiteAI within the meaning of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For privacy inquiries, contact: privacy@pastesuiteai.com

Data Stored on Your Device

PasteSuiteAI stores the following data locally in your user directory (~/.pastesuiteai/):

  • Settings — Your preferences, action configurations, and prompt library entries. Stored in settings.json.
  • Action definitions — Your custom and built-in action configurations. Stored in actions.json.
  • Usage data — Timestamps of AI actions for license enforcement (rolling 24-hour window). Stored in usage.json. No content is recorded.
  • Application log — Diagnostic log for troubleshooting. Stored in app.log. Contains no clipboard content or AI responses.
  • API keys — Stored in your operating system's secure credential store (Windows Credential Manager / macOS Keychain). Never in plaintext files.
  • License key — Stored in your operating system's secure credential store. Verified offline via cryptographic signature.

Data Sent to Third Parties

PasteSuiteAI sends data to external services only when you manually trigger an AI action. Specifically:

  • What is sent: The text you selected or copied (clipboard content), combined with the action's prompt template and any additional input you typed.
  • When: Only when you press a hotkey or click an action. Never automatically, never in the background.
  • Where: To the AI provider you configured (e.g. OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, Anthropic, or a local model). The connection is direct from your device to the provider — there is no PasteSuiteAI server in between.
  • Authentication: Using the API key you provided, stored in your OS credential store.

PasteSuiteAI itself never receives, stores, or has access to your text content or AI responses. We cannot see what you send or receive.

No Tracking, No Analytics, No Cookies

The PasteSuiteAI application and this website do not use cookies, analytics services, tracking pixels, or any form of telemetry. No data is sent to PasteSuiteAI servers because no such servers exist.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. The application provides built-in tools to exercise your rights:

  • Right of access & portability (Art. 15, 20) — Use the "Export My Data" feature in Settings to download all your data as a JSON file.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — Use the "Delete All Data" feature in Settings to permanently remove all local data, including API keys and license keys from the OS credential store.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — Edit your settings, connections, and actions directly in the application at any time.

Since no data is stored on our servers, there is nothing to request from us. You can exercise all rights directly on your device.

Data Retention

  • Action history: Maximum 15 entries, cleared on every app restart.
  • Usage timestamps: Rolling window, automatically pruned after 7 days.
  • Application log: Configurable maximum line count (default: 500 lines). Can be disabled entirely in Settings.

Children

PasteSuiteAI is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Significant changes will be noted in the application's changelog.

Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact: privacy@pastesuiteai.com
