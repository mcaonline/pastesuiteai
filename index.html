Frequently asked.

Which AI providers are supported?

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Mistral, Azure OpenAI, Azure AI Inference, xAI Grok, Groq, DeepSeek, Qwen, Zhipu, Doubao, Ernie, Hunyuan, MiniMax, Moonshot, Stepfun, Baichuan, SiliconFlow, Cohere, Together, Fireworks, Cerebras, SambaNova, HuggingFace, Perplexity, OpenRouter, plus ElevenLabs and iFlytek for speech. Local servers: Ollama, LM Studio, Foundry Local, Jan, GPT4All, vLLM, LocalAI, llama.cpp, and any other OpenAI-compatible endpoint. You can also define your own Custom API pipelines. The list keeps growing — see the Connections docs for the current set.

Do you see my data or prompts?

No. PasteSuiteAI sends requests directly from your machine to the provider you chose. We do not operate any proxy, inference, or analytics backend. Your API keys are held only in your Windows Credential Manager.

Can I use it fully offline?

Yes. Configure a local model via Ollama or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, and use a local STT provider. Nothing leaves your machine. License verification is also offline — no phone-home on activation.

What does the Pro license include?

An annual key that removes the free-tier usage cap (15 AI actions per 24h) so you get unlimited AI actions. One key is valid across your devices. The subscription renews automatically each year; you can cancel anytime in the Paddle Buyer Portal (link in your purchase confirmation email) and your current paid year stays active until its end. See Licensing & Trial for full details.

Is there a Mac or Linux version?

Not today. PasteSuiteAI is Windows-only and deeply integrated with Windows input, clipboard, and credential APIs. A cross-platform port is not on the short-term roadmap.

What happens to my data when I uninstall?

API keys in the OS credential store are removed by the uninstaller. Settings and history live in ~/.pastesuiteai/ and can be deleted manually. No cloud data exists to clean up.
