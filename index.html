Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

A focused tool that does a few things exceptionally well.

AI Text Transforms

Fix grammar, translate, summarize, rephrase, or run custom prompts on any selected text. Configure actions with your own templates.

Speech Transcription

Record audio with a hotkey and instantly transcribe and transform text using your configured speech-to-text provider. Paste anywhere.

Text Paste & Prompt Library

Paste as plain text, or store prompts and templates to insert into any AI tool or app with a hotkey.

Custom Hotkeys

Assign any keyboard shortcut to any action. Trigger transforms, recordings, or paste actions without leaving your workflow.

Multiple Providers

Connect to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Switch providers per action or globally.

Secure by Design

API keys stored in your OS keychain (Windows Credential Manager / macOS Keychain). No PasteSuiteAI server, no telemetry. Data goes only to your chosen AI provider.
